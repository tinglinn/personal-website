TING LIN

Hi! I'm Ting, a programmer, writer and journalist from Guangzhou/Toronto. I currently study CS + Linguistics at Stanford University, where I do research in the Stanford NLP Group within Stanford AI Lab.

My writing on art, politics and digital culture have appeared in The Baffler, Sixth Tone, Heichi Magazine and more. I am a part of Chaoyang Trap, an experimential newsletter about life on the Chinese internet.

Current interests: NLP for sinitic languages (esp. Cantonese), human-computer interaction, media technology, language preservation
