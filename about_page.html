TING LIN

Ting Lin is a writer, journalist and programmer from Guangzhou/Toronto. She currently studies CS + Linguistics at Stanford University, where she does research in the Stanford NLP Group within Stanford AI Lab.

Her writing on art, politics and digital culture have appeared in The Baffler, Sixth Tone, Heichi Magazine and more. She is a part of Chaoyang Trap, an experimential newsletter about life on the Chinese internet.

Current interests: human-computer interaction, media technology, language preservation, HAI
